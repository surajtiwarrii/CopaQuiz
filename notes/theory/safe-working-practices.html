परिचय & Workplace SafetyIntroduction & Workplace Safety

Safe Working Practices क्या हैं?

What are Safe Working Practices?

Safe Working Practices वो rules और guidelines हैं जो workplace पर accidents, injuries, और health hazards को रोकने के लिए follow किए जाते हैं — computer lab में भी इन्हें follow करना ज़रूरी है।

Safe Working Practices are the rules and guidelines followed at the workplace to prevent accidents, injuries, and health hazards — equally important in a computer lab.

Safety क्यों ज़रूरी है?

Why is Safety Important?

  • Accidents और injuries से बचाव
  • Equipment की life बढ़ाना
  • Data और software की सुरक्षा
  • Healthy work environment बनाए रखना
  • Legal compliance (laws का पालन)
  • Prevention of accidents and injuries
  • Extending the life of equipment
  • Protection of data and software
  • Maintaining a healthy work environment
  • Legal compliance (following laws)

Types of Hazards

Types of Hazards

Hazard TypeमतलबMeaning
PhysicalSlips, falls, noise, fireSlips, falls, noise, fire
ElectricalElectric shock, short circuit, overloadingElectric shock, short circuit, overloading
Ergonomicगलत posture, RSI (Repetitive Strain Injury)Wrong posture, RSI (Repetitive Strain Injury)
Cyber/DataVirus, data loss, unauthorized accessVirus, data loss, unauthorized access
EnvironmentalE-waste, radiation from screensE-waste, radiation from screens
याद रखें: Safety सिर्फ physical नहीं होती — digital safety (data, password, software) भी उतनी ही ज़रूरी है।Remember: safety is not only physical — digital safety (data, password, software) is equally important.

Computer Lab Safety RulesComputer Lab Safety Rules

Do's — ये ज़रूर करें

Do's — Always Follow These

  • Lab में enter करने से पहले permission लें
  • Computer को properly Shut Down करके छोड़ें
  • Wires और cables को organized रखें
  • Lab छोड़ने से पहले computer OFF करें
  • Antivirus software updated रखें
  • Pen drive use करने से पहले scan करें
  • Data का नियमित backup लें
  • Lab में proper footwear पहनें (rubber sole बेहतर)
  • Take permission before entering the lab
  • Always properly Shut Down the computer before leaving
  • Keep wires and cables organized
  • Turn OFF the computer before leaving the lab
  • Keep antivirus software updated
  • Scan pen drives before use
  • Take regular data backups
  • Wear proper footwear in the lab (rubber soles better)

Don'ts — ये कभी न करें

Don'ts — Never Do These

  • Lab में खाना-पीना लेकर न जाएं
  • बिना permission किसी का computer use न करें
  • खुले पानी/liquids को computer के पास न रखें
  • Computer को एकदम बंद (Power off) न करें — हमेशा Shut Down करें
  • Unknown USB/Pen drive insert न करें
  • Pirated software install न करें
  • Computer पर lean/झुककर न बैठें
  • Monitor को छुएं/touch न करें
  • Never bring food/drinks into the lab
  • Never use someone else's computer without permission
  • Never keep open water/liquids near computers
  • Never force-cut power — always Shut Down properly
  • Never insert unknown USB/pen drives
  • Never install pirated software
  • Never lean on or slump over the computer
  • Never touch/tap the monitor screen
Exam Tip: Computer Lab Safety Rules के Do's और Don'ts की list exam में अक्सर पूछी जाती है — कम से कम 5-5 याद करें।Exam Tip: Lists of Do's and Don'ts of Computer Lab Safety are often asked in exams — memorize at least 5 of each.

Electrical SafetyElectrical Safety

Electrical Safety क्यों ज़रूरी है?

Why is Electrical Safety Important?

Computer पूरी तरह electricity पर depend करता है — improper handling से electric shock, short circuit, fire, या equipment damage हो सकता है।

Computers fully depend on electricity — improper handling can cause electric shock, short circuits, fire, or equipment damage.

Key Electrical Safety Rules

Key Electrical Safety Rules

  • सभी electrical connections proper earthing (grounding) के साथ होने चाहिए
  • Overloading से बचें — एक plug point पर बहुत सारे devices न लगाएं
  • Damaged/frayed wires को तुरंत replace करें
  • Wet hands से electrical equipment कभी न छुएं
  • Computer repair/servicing से पहले power supply बंद करें
  • UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) use करें — sudden power cut से बचाता है
  • Surge protector/spike guard use करें — voltage fluctuation से बचाता है
  • Fuse/MCB (Miniature Circuit Breaker) का proper use करें
  • All electrical connections must have proper earthing (grounding)
  • Avoid overloading — don't plug too many devices into one outlet
  • Replace damaged/frayed wires immediately
  • Never touch electrical equipment with wet hands
  • Always turn off power before repairing/servicing a computer
  • Use a UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) — protects from sudden power cuts
  • Use a surge protector/spike guard — protects from voltage fluctuations
  • Properly use Fuse/MCB (Miniature Circuit Breaker)

महत्वपूर्ण Terms

Important Terms

TermमतलबMeaning
Earthing/GroundingExtra current को earth में safely discharge करनाSafely discharging excess current into the earth

FuseExcess current आने पर खुद जल जाता है, circuit protect करता हैBurns out on excess current, protects the circuit

याद रखें: Electric shock की situation में — पहले power switch OFF करें, तब victim को छुएं — direct touch मत करें, वरना आपको भी shock लग सकता है।Remember: in case of electric shock — first turn OFF the power switch, then touch the victim — never touch directly, or you may get shocked too.

Fire Triangle

Fire Triangle

आग लगने के लिए तीन चीज़ें ज़रूरी हैं — Fuel (ईंधन) + Oxygen (हवा) + Heat (गर्मी)। इनमें से कोई एक हटा दो, आग बुझ जाएगी।

Three things are needed for fire — Fuel + Oxygen + Heat. Remove any one of them and the fire will go out.

Ergonomics & PostureErgonomics & Posture

Ergonomics क्या है?

What is Ergonomics?

Ergonomics workplace को इंसान के शरीर के हिसाब से design करने की science है — ताकि काम comfortable हो, injuries न हों, और productivity बढ़े।

Ergonomics is the science of designing the workplace to suit the human body — so work is comfortable, injuries are prevented, and productivity increases.

Correct Workstation Setup

Correct Workstation Setup

PartPartCorrect PositionCorrect Position
ChairChairपीठ सीधी, feet flat on floor, hips at 90°Back straight, feet flat on floor, hips at 90°
MonitorMonitorEye level पर, 45-70 cm दूरAt eye level, 45-70 cm away
KeyboardKeyboardElbows 90° angle पर, wrists straightElbows at 90°, wrists straight
MouseMouseKeyboard के बगल में, same level परNext to keyboard, at the same level
LightingLightingNatural light side से आए, monitor पर glare न होNatural light from the side, no glare on monitor

RSI — Repetitive Strain Injury

RSI — Repetitive Strain Injury

RSI एक ही motion बार-बार करने से muscles और nerves में injury है — जैसे लंबे समय तक typing करने से wrists में दर्द (Carpal Tunnel Syndrome)।

RSI is an injury to muscles and nerves from repeating the same motion — like wrist pain from prolonged typing (Carpal Tunnel Syndrome).

Break Routine

Break Routine

हर 1-2 घंटे काम के बाद 5-10 minutes का break लें — stretching करें, चलें, हाथ-पैर हिलाएं। लंबे समय तक एक ही position में बैठना harmful होता है।

Take a 5-10 minute break after every 1-2 hours of work — stretch, walk, and move your hands and legs. Sitting in one position for too long is harmful.

याद रखें: Monitor की brightness और contrast comfortable level पर रखें — बहुत ज़्यादा brightness से आंखें थक जाती हैं।Remember: keep monitor brightness and contrast at a comfortable level — too much brightness tires the eyes.

Eye Care & Health HazardsEye Care & Health Hazards

Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS)

Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS)

CVS लंबे समय तक computer screen देखने से होने वाली eye और vision problems का group है — जैसे आंखें थकना, जलन, headache, blurred vision।

CVS is a group of eye and vision problems caused by prolonged computer screen use — such as eye fatigue, burning, headache, blurred vision.

20-20-20 Rule

20-20-20 Rule

हर 20 minutes काम के बाद, 20 feet (6 मीटर) दूर किसी चीज़ को 20 seconds तक देखें — eye strain कम होती है।

Every 20 minutes of work, look at something 20 feet (6 metres) away for 20 seconds — reduces eye strain.

Eye Care Tips

Eye Care Tips

  • Monitor को eye level पर रखें
  • Monitor से 45-70 cm दूरी रखें
  • Anti-glare screen/glasses use करें
  • Proper lighting रखें — monitor पर glare न हो
  • Screen की brightness comfortable level पर रखें
  • पलकें (blink) ज़्यादा झपकाएं — screen देखते समय blinking कम हो जाती है
  • Keep the monitor at eye level
  • Maintain a distance of 45-70 cm from the monitor
  • Use anti-glare screens/glasses
  • Ensure proper lighting — no glare on the monitor
  • Set screen brightness to a comfortable level
  • Blink more often — blinking decreases when staring at a screen

Hardware Handling SafetyHardware Handling Safety

Hardware Handling के Rules

Hardware Handling Rules

  • Hardware install/remove करने से पहले computer बंद करें और power unplug करें
  • ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) से बचने के लिए Anti-static wrist strap use करें
  • Components को किनारों से पकड़ें, circuit board को directly touch न करें
  • Hard disk को shock/vibration से बचाएं
  • Monitor/LCD को surface पर रखने से पहले cloth बिछाएं
  • Printer में paper jam को carefully हटाएं — force न लगाएं
  • Cleaning के लिए dry/anti-static cloth use करें — chemicals avoid करें
  • Hardware को moisture/liquid से दूर रखें
  • Turn off and unplug the computer before installing/removing hardware
  • Use an anti-static wrist strap to prevent ESD (Electrostatic Discharge)
  • Hold components by the edges — never touch circuit boards directly
  • Protect hard disks from shock/vibration
  • Lay a cloth before placing a monitor/LCD on a surface
  • Remove printer paper jams carefully — never force them
  • Use a dry/anti-static cloth for cleaning — avoid chemicals
  • Keep hardware away from moisture/liquid

ESD (Electrostatic Discharge)

ESD (Electrostatic Discharge)

ESD body में build-up हुई static electricity का sudden discharge है — यह computer components को damage कर सकती है। इससे बचने के लिए Anti-static wrist strap या metal surface touch करने के बाद components handle करें।

ESD is the sudden discharge of static electricity built up in the body — it can damage computer components. Prevent it by using an anti-static wrist strap or touching a metal surface before handling components.

याद रखें: CPU cabinet खोलते समय हमेशा power OFF करें और कम से कम 5 minutes बाद खोलें — capacitors discharge होने में time लगता है।Remember: always power OFF and wait at least 5 minutes before opening a CPU cabinet — capacitors take time to discharge.

Data Safety & BackupData Safety & Backup

Data Safety क्यों ज़रूरी है?

Why is Data Safety Important?

Data किसी भी organization का सबसे valuable asset होता है — virus, hardware failure, human error, या theft से data permanently loss हो सकता है।

Data is the most valuable asset of any organization — virus, hardware failure, human error, or theft can cause permanent data loss.

Data Safety के उपाय

Data Safety Measures

MeasureकामPurpose
Password ProtectionStrong password से unauthorized access रोकनाPreventing unauthorized access with strong passwords
Antivirus SoftwareVirus, malware से data की सुरक्षाProtecting data from viruses and malware
Regular UpdatesOS और software updates से security patches मिलते हैंOS and software updates provide security patches
EncryptionData को unreadable code में convert करनाConverting data into unreadable code
Access Controlसिर्फ authorized users को data access देनाGiving data access only to authorized users

Backup क्या है?

What is Backup?

Backup data की एक duplicate/extra copy है जो किसी safe/different location पर रखी जाती है — original data खो जाने पर restore किया जा सकता है।

A Backup is a duplicate/extra copy of data stored in a safe/different location — it can be restored if the original data is lost.

Types of Backup

Types of Backup

TypeमतलबMeaning
Full Backupपूरे data का complete backup, हर बारComplete backup of all data, every time
Incremental Backupसिर्फ last backup के बाद बदले हुए data का backupBackup of only data changed since the last backup
Differential BackupLast Full backup के बाद बदले सभी data का backupBackup of all data changed since the last full backup

3-2-1 Backup Rule

3-2-1 Backup Rule

Data की 3 copies, 2 अलग-अलग media पर, जिसमें से 1 off-site (अलग location) पर हो।

Keep 3 copies of data, on 2 different media, with 1 stored off-site (different location).

याद रखें: Backup को regularly test करें — backup है या नहीं, यह सिर्फ restore करके पता चलता है।Remember: regularly test your backup — you only know it works when you can actually restore it.

Software Licensing & E-WasteSoftware Licensing & E-Waste

Software License क्या है?

What is a Software License?

Software License एक legal agreement है जो define करता है कि software को कैसे use, copy, और distribute किया जा सकता है — बिना license software use करना illegal है।

A Software License is a legal agreement defining how software may be used, copied, and distributed — using software without a license is illegal.

Types of Software License

Types of Software License

TypeमतलबMeaning
ProprietaryPaid software, source code closed (जैसे MS Office)Paid software, closed source code (like MS Office)
FreewareFree use, पर source code नहीं मिलताFree to use, but source code not available
SharewareLimited time/features free trial, फिर pay करना पड़ता हैFree trial with limited time/features, then requires payment
Open SourceFree + source code available, modify कर सकते हैं (जैसे Linux)Free + source code available, can be modified (like Linux)

Software Piracy क्या है?

What is Software Piracy?

Software Piracy बिना license के software को copy, distribute, या use करना है — यह illegal है और IT Act के तहत दंडनीय है।

Software Piracy is copying, distributing, or using software without a license — it is illegal and punishable under the IT Act.

E-Waste क्या है?

What is E-Waste?

E-Waste (Electronic Waste) पुराने/खराब electronic devices हैं जैसे old computers, monitors, mobiles, printers। इनमें hazardous materials (lead, mercury) होते हैं।

E-Waste (Electronic Waste) consists of old/broken electronic devices like old computers, monitors, mobiles, printers. These contain hazardous materials (lead, mercury).

E-Waste का Safe Disposal

Safe Disposal of E-Waste

  • Authorized E-Waste collection centers पर जमा करें
  • Electronics को regular garbage में न फेंकें
  • पुराने devices donate/refurbish करें
  • Manufacturer take-back programs use करें
  • Deposit at authorized E-Waste collection centres
  • Never throw electronics in regular garbage
  • Donate/refurbish old devices
  • Use manufacturer take-back programs
याद रखें: Pirated software use करने पर legal action हो सकता है और security risk भी रहता है — हमेशा licensed software ही use करें।Remember: using pirated software can lead to legal action and creates security risks — always use licensed software.
