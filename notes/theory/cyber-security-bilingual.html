Cyber Security का परिचयIntroduction to Cyber Security

Cyber Security क्या है?

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security computers, networks, programs, और data को unauthorized access, attacks, और damage से बचाने की practice है।

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, programs, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.

CIA Triad — Security के 3 Pillars

CIA Triad — 3 Pillars of Security

TermमतलबMeaning
Confidentialityसिर्फ authorized लोगों को data access मिलेOnly authorized people get access to data
IntegrityData accurate और unaltered रहेData remains accurate and unaltered
AvailabilityData/system ज़रूरत पड़ने पर हमेशा available रहेData/system remains available whenever needed

Cyber Security क्यों ज़रूरी है?

Why is Cyber Security Important?

  • Personal data (banking, identity) की सुरक्षा
  • Business data और reputation की सुरक्षा
  • Financial losses से बचाव
  • National security और critical infrastructure की सुरक्षा
  • Protecting personal data (banking, identity)
  • Protecting business data and reputation
  • Preventing financial losses
  • Protecting national security and critical infrastructure

Cyber Crime क्या है?

What is Cyber Crime?

Cyber Crime कोई भी illegal activity है जो computer/internet का use करके की जाती है — जैसे hacking, fraud, data theft।

Cyber Crime is any illegal activity carried out using a computer/internet — like hacking, fraud, data theft.

याद रखें: CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) हर Cyber Security course का सबसे basic और सबसे ज़्यादा पूछा जाने वाला concept है। Remember: the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) is the most basic and most frequently asked concept in any Cyber Security course.

Malware (Virus, Worm, Trojan)Malware (Virus, Worm, Trojan)

Malware क्या है?

What is Malware?

Malware (Malicious Software) कोई भी harmful program है जो system को नुकसान पहुंचाने, data चुराने, या unauthorized access पाने के लिए design किया जाता है।

Malware (Malicious Software) is any harmful program designed to damage a system, steal data, or gain unauthorized access.

Virus vs Worm vs Trojan

Virus vs Worm vs Trojan

TypeविशेषताFeature
Virusकिसी file से attach होता है, फैलने के लिए human action चाहिएAttaches to a file, needs human action to spread
Wormखुद-ब-खुद फैलता है, network में बिना human action केSelf-replicates and spreads across networks without human action
Trojan Horseकिसी useful program जैसा दिखता है, पर अंदर harmful code होता हैDisguises as a useful program but contains harmful code

अन्य Malware Types

Other Malware Types

TypeमतलबMeaning
SpywareUser की activity को secretly track करता हैSecretly tracks a user's activity
Adwareअनचाहे ads दिखाता हैDisplays unwanted ads
KeyloggerKeyboard के हर keystroke को record करता हैRecords every keystroke on the keyboard
RansomwareFiles को encrypt करके ransom मांगता हैEncrypts files and demands ransom

Antivirus Software

Antivirus Software

Antivirus malware को detect, block, और remove करने वाला software है — system को regularly scan करता है और known threats को database के through पहचानता है।

Antivirus software detects, blocks, and removes malware — it regularly scans the system and identifies known threats via a database.

याद रखें: Virus को फैलने के लिए host file चाहिए और human action (जैसे file open करना) ज़रूरी है, पर Worm बिना किसी human action के खुद फैल सकता है। Remember: a Virus needs a host file and human action (like opening a file) to spread, but a Worm can spread on its own without any human action.

Social Engineering AttacksSocial Engineering Attacks

Social Engineering क्या है?

What is Social Engineering?

Social Engineering technical hacking की जगह human psychology का use करके लोगों को सही information देने या गलत action लेने के लिए manipulate करता है।

Social Engineering manipulates human psychology — instead of technical hacking — to trick people into giving sensitive information or taking incorrect actions.

Common Social Engineering Techniques

Common Social Engineering Techniques

TechniqueमतलबMeaning
PhishingFake email/SMS से sensitive details चुरानाStealing sensitive details via fake email/SMS
VishingPhone call के through फ्रॉड करना (Voice Phishing)Committing fraud via phone call (Voice Phishing)
SmishingSMS के through फ्रॉड करनाCommitting fraud via SMS
Pretextingझूठी story बनाकर information निकलवानाCreating a false story to extract information
Baitingकोई attractive चीज़ (free offer/USB) से लालच देनाLuring with something attractive (free offer/USB)
Shoulder Surfingपीछे से देखकर password/PIN चुरानाStealing a password/PIN by looking over someone's shoulder

Phishing कैसे पहचानें

How to Identify Phishing

  • Urgent/threatening language (जैसे "Account suspend हो जाएगा")
  • Sender का email address अजीब/misspelled लगे
  • Unexpected attachment या link
  • Grammar/spelling की गलतियां
  • Urgent/threatening language (like "Your account will be suspended")
  • Sender's email address looks odd/misspelled
  • Unexpected attachment or link
  • Grammar/spelling mistakes
Exam Tip: Social Engineering का मुख्य target technology नहीं बल्कि इंसान (human) होता है — इसलिए इसे "Human Hacking" भी कहते हैं। Exam Tip: the main target of Social Engineering is not technology but humans — that's why it's also called "Human Hacking".

Unit 05 · सुरक्षा उपायUnit 05 · Security Measures

Network SecurityNetwork Security

Network Security क्या है?

What is Network Security?

Network Security किसी computer network को unauthorized access, misuse, और attacks से बचाने की practices और tools का set है।

Network Security is a set of practices and tools that protect a computer network from unauthorized access, misuse, and attacks.

Firewall

Firewall

Firewall network और बाहरी दुनिया के बीच एक barrier की तरह काम करता है — incoming/outgoing traffic को rules के according monitor और control करता है।

A Firewall acts as a barrier between a network and the outside world — monitoring and controlling incoming/outgoing traffic based on rules.

VPN (Virtual Private Network)

VPN (Virtual Private Network)

VPN internet connection को encrypt करके एक secure, private tunnel बनाता है — public network पर भी data को safe रखता है और IP address छुपाता है।

A VPN encrypts an internet connection, creating a secure, private tunnel — keeps data safe even on public networks and hides the IP address.

IDS & IPS

IDS & IPS

TermकामPurpose
IDS (Intrusion Detection System)Suspicious activity को detect करके alert करता हैDetects suspicious activity and alerts
IPS (Intrusion Prevention System)Suspicious activity को detect करके automatically block करता हैDetects suspicious activity and automatically blocks it

Secure Wi-Fi Practices

Secure Wi-Fi Practices

  • Strong WPA3/WPA2 encryption use करें
  • Default router password ज़रूर बदलें
  • Public Wi-Fi पर sensitive transactions न करें
  • Use strong WPA3/WPA2 encryption
  • Always change the default router password
  • Avoid sensitive transactions on public Wi-Fi
याद रखें: Firewall traffic को filter करता है (gatekeeper), जबकि VPN data को encrypt करके privacy देता है — दोनों अलग-अलग layer पर काम करते हैं। Remember: a Firewall filters traffic (acts as a gatekeeper), while a VPN provides privacy by encrypting data — both work at different layers.

Cryptography & EncryptionCryptography & Encryption

Cryptography क्या है?

What is Cryptography?

Cryptography data को secret codes में बदलने की science है, ताकि सिर्फ authorized person ही उसे पढ़ सके।

Cryptography is the science of converting data into secret codes so that only an authorized person can read it.

Encryption & Decryption

Encryption & Decryption

TermमतलबMeaning

CiphertextEncrypted, unreadable dataEncrypted, unreadable data
DecryptionCiphertext को वापस readable plaintext में बदलनाConverting ciphertext back into readable plaintext

Symmetric vs Asymmetric Encryption

Symmetric vs Asymmetric Encryption

SymmetricAsymmetric

तेज़, पर key share करना riskyFaster, but sharing the key is riskyधीमा, पर ज़्यादा secureSlower, but more secure

Digital Signature

Digital Signature

Digital Signature किसी document/message की authenticity और integrity verify करने का electronic तरीका है — handwritten signature का digital equivalent।

A Digital Signature is an electronic way to verify a document/message's authenticity and integrity — the digital equivalent of a handwritten signature.

याद रखें: https:// websites SSL/TLS encryption use करती हैं, जो asymmetric और symmetric दोनों तरीकों को combine करती है। Remember: https:// websites use SSL/TLS encryption, which combines both asymmetric and symmetric methods.

Authentication & Password SecurityAuthentication & Password Security

Authentication क्या है?

What is Authentication?

Authentication किसी user की identity verify करने की process है — "आप वही हैं जो आप कह रहे हैं" यह confirm करना।

Authentication is the process of verifying a user's identity — confirming "you are who you say you are".

Authentication Factors

Authentication Factors

FactorउदाहरणExample
कुछ जो आप जानते हैंSomething you knowPassword, PIN
कुछ जो आपके पास हैSomething you haveOTP, Security Token
कुछ जो आप हैंSomething you areFingerprint, Face ID

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA security की एक extra layer है, जिसमें password के अलावा एक और verification step (जैसे OTP) चाहिए होता है — सिर्फ password चुराने से account access नहीं मिलता।

2FA adds an extra security layer requiring a second verification step (like OTP) besides the password — stealing just the password isn't enough to access the account.

Strong Password बनाने के Rules

Rules for a Strong Password

  • कम से कम 8-12 characters लंबा हो
  • Uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols का mix हो
  • Personal info (नाम, जन्मतिथि) avoid करें
  • हर account के लिए अलग password use करें
  • Regularly password बदलते रहें
  • At least 8-12 characters long
  • Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
  • Avoid personal info (name, date of birth)
  • Use a different password for each account
  • Change passwords regularly
याद रखें: Authentication (कौन हो) और Authorization (क्या कर सकते हो) दो अलग concepts हैं — exam में confuse न हों। Remember: Authentication (who you are) and Authorization (what you can do) are two different concepts — don't confuse them in exams.

Cyber Security Best PracticesCyber Security Best Practices

Personal Cyber Hygiene

Personal Cyber Hygiene

  • Software/OS को regularly update करें (security patches के लिए)
  • Unknown links/attachments पर click न करें
  • Trusted antivirus install और updated रखें
  • Important data का regular backup लें
  • Social media पर personal info limit करें
  • Regularly update software/OS (for security patches)
  • Don't click unknown links/attachments
  • Install and keep trusted antivirus updated
  • Take regular backups of important data
  • Limit personal info shared on social media

Organization-Level Practices

Organization-Level Practices

PracticeकामPurpose
Employee TrainingStaff को security awareness सिखानाTeaching staff security awareness
Access Controlसिर्फ ज़रूरी लोगों को ज़रूरी data access देनाGiving access only to those who need it
Regular AuditsSecurity gaps को periodically check करनाPeriodically checking for security gaps
Incident Response PlanAttack होने पर क्या करना है, उसकी पहले से planningPre-planning what to do if an attack occurs

Backup Strategy — 3-2-1 Rule

Backup Strategy — 3-2-1 Rule

Data की 3 copies रखें, 2 अलग-अलग storage types पर, और 1 copy off-site (अलग location) पर — Ransomware और data loss से बचने का best तरीका।

Keep 3 copies of data, on 2 different storage types, with 1 copy off-site (different location) — the best way to protect against ransomware and data loss.

Exam Tip: Cyber Security में सबसे weak link हमेशा "human error" होता है — इसलिए awareness और training सबसे important practice मानी जाती है। Exam Tip: in Cyber Security, the weakest link is always "human error" — that's why awareness and training are considered the most important practice.

Unit 09 · कानूनUnit 09 · Law

Cyber Laws & IT ActCyber Laws & IT Act

Cyber Law क्या है?

What is Cyber Law?

Cyber Law वो legal rules हैं जो Internet, computers, और digital transactions से जुड़े crimes और disputes को regulate करते हैं।

Cyber Law refers to the legal rules that regulate crimes and disputes related to the Internet, computers, and digital transactions.

IT Act 2000 (भारत)

IT Act 2000 (India)

भारत में Information Technology Act, 2000 मुख्य कानून है जो electronic governance, digital signatures, और cyber crimes को cover करता है।

In India, the Information Technology Act, 2000 is the primary law covering electronic governance, digital signatures, and cyber crimes.

महत्वपूर्ण IT Act Sections

Important IT Act Sections

Sectionकिसके बारे मेंAbout

Cyber Crime की Reporting

Reporting Cyber Crime

भारत में Cyber Crime की complaint National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) या local police cyber cell पर की जा सकती है।

In India, cyber crime complaints can be filed via the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or the local police cyber cell.

याद रखें: IT Act 2000 भारत का पहला cyber law है, और इसमें Section 66 hacking से directly जुड़ा हुआ सबसे important section माना जाता है। Remember: IT Act 2000 is India's first cyber law, and Section 66 is considered the most important section directly related to hacking.
