E-Commerce का परिचयIntroduction to E-Commerce

E-Commerce क्या है?

What is E-Commerce?

E-Commerce (Electronic Commerce) Internet के through products और services को buy/sell करने की प्रक्रिया है — payment, order, delivery सब online होता है।

E-Commerce (Electronic Commerce) is the process of buying/selling products and services over the Internet — payment, ordering, and delivery all happen online.

E-Commerce का इतिहास (संक्षेप में)

History of E-Commerce (Brief)

E-Commerce की शुरुआत 1990s में हुई, जब Internet आम लोगों तक पहुंचा। Amazon (1994) और eBay (1995) जैसी companies ने online shopping को popular बनाया। भारत में Flipkart, Amazon India, Myntra जैसी websites प्रमुख हैं।

E-Commerce began in the 1990s when the Internet reached the general public. Companies like Amazon (1994) and eBay (1995) popularized online shopping. In India, prominent platforms include Flipkart, Amazon India, and Myntra.

याद रखें: E-Commerce एक narrower term है (सिर्फ transactions), जबकि E-Business एक broader term है जो पूरे online business operations को cover करता है। Remember: E-Commerce is a narrower term (only transactions), while E-Business is a broader term covering all online business operations.

E-Commerce Business ModelsE-Commerce Business Models

Business Model क्या है?

What is a Business Model?

Business Model बताता है कि कोई company पैसा कैसे कमाती है — products बेचकर, services देकर, या किसी और तरीके से।

A Business Model describes how a company makes money — by selling products, providing services, or other means.

मुख्य E-Commerce Models

Main E-Commerce Models

ModelकामDescription
Marketplaceअलग-अलग sellers एक platform पर अपने products बेचते हैं (जैसे Amazon, Flipkart)Multiple sellers sell products on one platform (like Amazon, Flipkart)
Inventory-BasedCompany खुद का stock रखती है और सीधे बेचती हैThe company holds its own stock and sells directly
DropshippingSeller stock नहीं रखता, order आने पर manufacturer/supplier सीधे customer को भेजता हैThe seller holds no stock; the manufacturer/supplier ships directly to the customer on order

Aggregatorकई service providers को एक platform पर जोड़ता है (जैसे Swiggy, Ola)Connects multiple service providers on one platform (like Swiggy, Ola)

Online Payment SystemsOnline Payment Systems

Online Payment System क्या है?

What is an Online Payment System?

Online Payment System वो electronic तरीका है जिससे buyer Internet के through seller को payment करता है, बिना cash exchange किए।

An Online Payment System is the electronic method by which a buyer pays a seller over the Internet, without exchanging cash.

Common Payment Methods

Common Payment Methods

MethodविवरणDescription
Credit/Debit CardCard से सीधे bank account/credit से paymentPayment directly from a bank account/credit via card
Net BankingBank की website से सीधे online paymentDirect online payment via the bank's website
UPI (Unified Payments Interface)Mobile से instant bank-to-bank transfer (जैसे PhonePe, GPay)Instant bank-to-bank transfer via mobile (like PhonePe, GPay)
Digital WalletApp में पैसे load करके payment (जैसे Paytm Wallet)Paying using money loaded into an app (like Paytm Wallet)
Cash on Delivery (COD)Product मिलने पर cash में paymentPaying in cash upon delivery of the product
EMIPayment को कई monthly installments में बांटनाSplitting payment into multiple monthly installments

Payment Gateway

Payment Gateway

Payment Gateway एक service है जो customer के payment details को securely process करके merchant के bank account तक पहुंचाती है — जैसे Razorpay, PayU, CCAvenue।

A Payment Gateway is a service that securely processes a customer's payment details and routes them to the merchant's bank account — like Razorpay, PayU, CCAvenue.

E-Commerce SecurityE-Commerce Security

E-Commerce Security क्यों ज़रूरी है?

Why is E-Commerce Security Important?

Online transactions में sensitive data (card details, personal info) involve होता है, इसलिए security सुनिश्चित करना ज़रूरी है ताकि fraud और data theft से बचा जा सके।

Online transactions involve sensitive data (card details, personal info), so ensuring security is essential to prevent fraud and data theft.

Marketplace पर Seller बनना

Becoming a Seller on a Marketplace

Amazon, Flipkart जैसी marketplaces पर seller बनने के लिए business registration (GST), bank account, और product listings की ज़रूरत होती है — खुद की website बनाने की ज़रूरत नहीं पड़ती।

Becoming a seller on marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart requires business registration (GST), a bank account, and product listings — no need to build your own website.

याद रखें: Marketplace seller बनना अपनी website बनाने से आसान और सस्ता है, पर Marketplace पर commission देनी पड़ती है और competition भी ज़्यादा होता है। Remember: becoming a marketplace seller is easier and cheaper than building your own website, but you pay commission and face more competition there.

Supply Chain & Order ManagementSupply Chain & Order Management

Supply Chain क्या है?

What is a Supply Chain?

Supply Chain वो पूरी process है जिससे product manufacturer से लेकर customer तक पहुंचता है — raw material, production, warehouse, delivery सब शामिल हैं।

A Supply Chain is the entire process by which a product reaches the customer from the manufacturer — including raw material, production, warehousing, and delivery.

Order Fulfillment Process

Order Fulfillment Process

  1. Customer order place करता है
  2. Payment verify होता है
  3. Warehouse से product pack किया जाता है
  4. Courier/logistics partner को सौंपा जाता है
  5. Product customer तक deliver होता है
  1. Customer places the order
  2. Payment is verified
  3. Product is packed at the warehouse
  4. Handed over to courier/logistics partner
  5. Product is delivered to the customer

Logistics & Warehousing

Logistics & Warehousing

TermमतलबMeaning
WarehouseProducts को delivery से पहले store करने की जगहA place to store products before delivery
LogisticsProducts को सही जगह, सही समय पर पहुंचाने की पूरी processThe entire process of getting products to the right place at the right time
Last-Mile DeliveryProduct का warehouse से customer के घर तक का final हिस्साThe final leg of delivery from warehouse to the customer's door

Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds

E-Commerce में Return Policy ज़रूरी है क्योंकि customer product को physically देखकर नहीं खरीदता — इसलिए product पसंद न आने पर return/refund का option दिया जाता है।

A Return Policy is essential in E-Commerce since the customer doesn't physically inspect the product before buying — so a return/refund option is offered if they're not satisfied.

याद रखें: Order Tracking customer को real-time पता चलने देता है कि उसका order किस stage पर है — यह trust बढ़ाने का अहम tool है। Remember: Order Tracking lets customers know in real time which stage their order is at — an important trust-building tool.
